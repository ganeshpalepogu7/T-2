Analytics Benchmarking

Key Takeaway: F1-Score and Recall are crucial because credit card fraud datasets are extremely imbalanced. Accuracy is misleadingly high (e.g. 99%+) for all models.

Receiver Operating Characteristic: Random Forest and Decision Tree exhibit high Area Under Curve (AUC-ROC), indicating excellent discriminative capacity.

Precision-Recall Curve: More indicative than ROC for highly imbalanced data. Note how the Random Forest retains high Precision even at elevated Recall thresholds.
